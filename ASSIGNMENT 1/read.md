
 Algorithmic Efficiency & Recursion Toolkit (AERT)
  Overview:

The Algorithmic Efficiency & Recursion Toolkit (AERT) is a Python-based mini project developed as part of the Data Structures – Unit 1 assignment.
This project demonstrates the fundamentals of data structures, recursion, and algorithmic complexity analysis through well-known problems such as factorial, Fibonacci, Tower of Hanoi, and binary search.

The toolkit focuses on correct recursive implementation, efficiency comparison, and clear understanding of algorithm paradigms like Divide & Conquer and Dynamic Programming.
